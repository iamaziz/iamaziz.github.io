Ebola Open Data Jam

Feb 24th, 2015

Last Saturday, Feb. 21, I attended an open data jam event at the civic center in NYC. The event was dedicated to help countries, like Sierra Leone and Liberia, fight Ebola. Although I did not contribute much (just a map of Sierra Leone to display the locations Ebola centers), I was excited to be with such awesome people.
